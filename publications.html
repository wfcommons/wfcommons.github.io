Citing WfCommons

When citing WfCommons, please use the following paper. You should also actually read that paper, as it provides a recent and general overview on the framework. 

R. Ferreira da Silva, L. Pottier, T. Coleman, E. Deelman, and H. Casanova, "WorkflowHub: Community Framework for Enabling Scientific Workflow Research and Development," in 2020 IEEE/ACM Workflows in Support of Large-Scale Science (WORKS), p. 49-56, 2020. DOI: 10.1109/WORKS51914.2020.00012
